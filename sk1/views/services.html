The inspiration to create this experience for you came from one of my favorite movies, the stylish classic, "Breakfast At Tiffany's" with Audrey Hepburn. Audrey (one of my favorite women) was an actress, fashion icon and philanthropist known for her beauty, elegance and grace.

In the movie, the Tiffany's jewelry store was Holly's escape; the place she went to feel entirely safe from the "mean reds" (a state of mind in which she felt that something bad may happen to her) that plagued her from time to time. Tiffany's was a place of refuge for Holly representing order, peace, security and stability. It gave her the feelings she would want to have in a home if she could ever find somewhere she belonged.

During your Divine Destination experience, where we will start the day together in front of Tiffany's, I want you to be able to connect to the elegance from Audrey's role as Holly and also her vulnerability, fears and dreams. I want you to start to feel your feminine classy inner beauty and become inspired to make your home to feel like your refuge... your safe, peaceful, organized and serene place like a Tiffany's store, so that you feel you always want to be there.

This very unique one day experience, meeting with me in elegant locations will make your spirit and the most feminine part of you feel like it has come alive. Oftentimes it takes getting out of your everyday environment and stepping into something that feels different and more glamorous, to get in touch with the most treasured part of you that may have been unacknowledged or to awaken a part of you that you didn't even know existed...

...the part of you that deserves and has been ACHING for a less cluttered life, feeling completely in your elegant feminine power, expressing all of who you are and comfortable doing so, no matter what others in your life say about it.

Once you experience this feeling inside of yourself after our day together and you return to your home, you may have a completely different perspective from which you will now much more easily be able to clear your clutter and create beautiful, organized sacred spaces, and with less fear, because you will now want your outside to align with your new feeling inside. You will now know what you truly want for you in your life, and you will want to do everything in your power to make it so, one step at a time.

This is what you will receive in this package:



We will meet in Divine Decluttering Style for an entire day in the heart of the most elegant shopping/dining/tourist area in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.

This full day will be custom designed especially for you and will include non-physical decluttering, planning for your physical decluttering for when you get back home and conscious creating, all while in a beautiful private setting.

You will be treated to fine dining and lots of feminine fun.

You will receive some very special gifts from me that you will put to good use when you get back home.

You will receive a 90 day decluttering plan that I will completely customize to meet your needs. This includes follow-up consultations over Skype and on the phone. Receiving support over a period of 90 days will help move you from one season into the next and make you feel comfortable knowing you have the support while you get used to the feelings of living with less clutter.
